IN THE HEART OF WAYANAD WITH OUTSTANDING VIEWS

Nestled in the heart of Wayanad, Silverwoods resorts is on the edge of a tranquil and beautiful Banasura sagar. It is a haven of warmth, tranquilly and rejuvenation. Bathed in brilliant sunshine and clear skies, itoffers stunning views of Banasura sagar and mountains.

A Short History of the Resort

Her Enduring Journey

Be a part of our journey from the untouched hillock to the exquisitely curated haven. Fused ideas of the nature friendly architect from Srilanka Mr. Anton Jacob and the contemporary architect Mr. Roopesh from Kerala has brought out this luxury as well as eco friendly resort in the midst of lush green nature. The theme terracotta and wood has blended well with the landscape. The custom made terracotta and the wooden elements from the wall pictures, eco friendly dustbins, from wooden furnitures to flower pots are designed under direct supervision of the promoters.

Endless Indoor and Outdoor Fun

A World of Endless Adventure Awaits You

The resort offers a comprehensive array of recreation and spa facilities designed to cater to guests seeking both relaxation and active pursuits. The infinity pool and kids’ pool, provide fun and relaxation for all ages. The spa and wellness center features massage rooms, steam room, and spaces for yoga and meditation, ensuring a holistic approach to well-being.
The fitness center is equipped with modern gym equipment, and offers various fitness classes and personal training services. Sports enthusiasts can enjoy various out door games, while game rooms, kids’ clubs, and cinema rooms cater to indoor entertainment needs. Outdoor activities such as nature trails, bicycle rentals, and adventure sports provide exciting ways to explore the beautiful surroundings.
Relaxation areas allow guests to unwind in comfort and style. Overall, the resort’s facilities are thoughtfully designed to offer a perfect blend of relaxation, recreation, and luxury for an unforgettable guest experience.
