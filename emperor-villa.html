Nestled on the shores of a serene lake, surrounded by lush greenery, lies the luxurious cottage designed for ultimate Relaxation and Comfort . This idyllic retreat offers breathtaking views of the tranquil waters from every angle. As you step onto the Spacious Balcony , you’re greeted by the soothing sight of the lake stretching out before you, its surface shimmering in the sunlight. Here, nestled in a secluded corner, awaits your own Private Jacuzzi , where you can immerse yourself in warm, bubbling waters while savouring panoramic views of the serene landscape.

Inside, the cottage exudes elegance and charm, with tasteful furnishings and luxurious amenities throughout. The private living space is a sanctuary of comfort, featuring plush seating and floor-to-ceiling windows that bathe the room in natural light and offers uninterrupted views of the tranquil surroundings.

The bedroom is a haven of serenity with a sumptuous King-Sized Bed dressed in crisp linens and adorned with soft pillows, ensuring a restful night's sleep. Wake up to the gentle sound of birdsong and the soft rustle of leaves, as sunlight filters through the trees and dances across the water.

In the ensuite bathroom, a Spacious Walk-in Shower offers a luxurious bath experience where you can unwind and pamper yourself in style.

Take a leisurely stroll along the forest trails, breathe in the fresh, clean air, and feel all your cares melt away in this peaceful oasis.

Whether you're seeking a Romantic Getaway or a serene retreat to recharge and rejuvenate, this lake-facing luxury cottage with its jacuzzi on the balcony and private living space offers the perfect blend of comfort, tranquillity, and natural beauty.

Dear guests, to ensure safety and comfort of your stay, we have clarified some guidelines.

  1. *Check-In and Check-Out*:
    • Check-in is at 2 PM; check-out is at 11 AM.
    • Late check-out may incur additional charges.

  2. *Settlement of Bills*:
    • Bills must be settled on presentation.
    • Cheques are not accepted.
    • All foreign nationals are requested to settle their bills in foreign currency only, in keeping with govt. regulations.

  3. *Quiet Hours*:
    • Quiet hours are from 10 PM to 7 AM.
    • Please respect other guests by keeping noise to a minimum during these times.
    • Disturbing other Guests is not allowed.

  4. *Occupancy Limits*:
    • Each room has a maximum occupancy limit; please adhere to it.
    • Extra guests may require additional charges.

  5. *No Smoking*:
    • Smoking is prohibited inside the rooms and other indoor areas.

  6. *Dress Code*:
    • The Guests are requested to co-operate with the management by wearing proper dresses.
    • Kindly avoid wearing lungies or nighties in the Public Areas of the resort.
    • Proper Swimming Costumes should be used while using the Pool.

  7. *Cloth Washing*:
    • No guests are allowed to do washing and drying of clothes and cooking inside the premises. The management will be forced to take strict action against the same if found any.
    • Laundry facility is available .

  8. *Housekeeping*:
    • Though the house keeping cleans your on daily basis after your permission, the sheets will be changed only if the tent card is placed on the bed and the towels will be changed if the tent card is placed in the vanity counter.
    • Please place the "Do Not Disturb" sign if you do not wish to be disturbed.

  9. *Use of Resort Facilities*:
    • Follow all posted rules and regulations for pools, gyms, spas, and other facilities.
    • Children must be supervised by an adult at all times.

  10. *Damage and Theft*:
    • The Guest will be held responsible for any loss or damage to the resort property caused by himself, his guests, his friends or any person for whom he is responsible.
    • Please report any damage or maintenance issues to the front desk immediately.
    • The management shall not be responsible for any injury to the guest or damage to his belongings.

  11. *Safety and Security*:
    • Keep your valuables in the room safe.
    • Report any suspicious activity to resort security.

  12. *Food and Beverage*:
    • Outside food and beverages is strictly restricted.
    • Minibar items will be charged to your account upon consumption.

  13. *Behaviour and Conduct*:
    • Respect other guests and resort staff.
    • Inappropriate behaviours, including excessive noise, drunkenness, or harassment, will not be tolerated.

  14. *Environmental Practices*:
    • Conserve water and energy by reusing towels and turning off lights and electric gadgets when not in use.
    • Follow recycling guidelines provided by the resort.

  15. *Possession of hazardous Goods *:
    • Bringing into the Resort and/or storing any article of combustible/hazardous nature and/or prohibited goods of objectionable nature is prohibited. The Guest shall be solely liable and responsible to the Management for any loss financial or other that may be caused by such articles or negligence and non- observance of any/all instructions.

  16. *Amendment of Rules*:
    • The management reserves to itself the right to add or alter or amend any of the above rules, terms and conditions.
    • The guest shall use all the facilities and services available at the resort with care and caution. The guest shall abide by the instructions placed by the management at various places in the resort premises.

  17. *Management's Right*:
    • It is agreed that the Guest will conduct himself/herself in a respectable manner and not cause any nuisance or annoyance within the resort premises.
    • The Guest is requested to observe, abide by, confirm to and be bound by all applicable acts and laws and the Government Rules and Regulations in force from time to time.

    ***These rules help ensure a pleasant and safe stay for all guests.***
