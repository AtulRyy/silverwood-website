Karlad - The Infinity Pool

…Repose & unwind…

Lounge with a book at Wayanad Silverwoods pool area overlooking the Banasura Peak. An exotic view from the pool side to Banasura Sagar and hills will enthrall your mind and eyes. We conduct pool side dinner and conferences under the backdrop of serene nature of Wayanad. Take the plunge into the Pool that beckons you with its clear azure waters in the scenic location.

We also have a Kiddie’s pool area which is the dedicated space designed for our youngest guests to splash, play and enjoy endless hours of water-fun.

Banasura – Dine & View

…Taste the Passion, Savor the Destination…

Our Multi-cuisine Restaurant serve lip smacking dishes of your choice, be it Continental, Mughlai, North Indian or South Indian. Specially trained hospitality team will be ready to serve you the special beverages, feasts and delicacies. Our Restaurant provides you the most stunning view of the woods while relishing on the delicious treats.

Our team of chefs takes pride in using the freshest local ingredients, blending traditional recipes with modern techniques to create a menu that delights and surprises. Whether you’re enjoying a leisurely breakfast buffet, a sophisticated lunch, or an exquisite dinner, our restaurant offers an inviting setting where every meal becomes an occasion.

Ayur Sparsh – Ayurvedic Spa

Embedded within the serene surroundings of our resort, the spa offers a sanctuary where you can unwind, destress and revitalize your mind, body and spirit. Our professional therapists and state- of- the- art facilities ensure an exceptional spa experience tailored to your individual needs. Embrace the essence of holistic wellness as you indulge in our luxurious spa experiences, set against the backdrop of nature’s beauty.

Lakkidi – The Club House

…Where Memories are Made, and Laughter Echoes…

Welcome to the heart of our resort, Lakkidi -The Clubhouse – your ultimate retreat for relaxation and camaraderie. Step into a world where laughter fills the air, and every moment is an opportunity for connection and celebration. Our Clubhouse offers a perfect blend of elegance and comfort, featuring plush seating areas, an inviting bar stocked with top-shelf spirits, and a variety of recreational activities to suit all tastes. Whether you're here to unwind with a drink, engage in friendly competition with a game of billiards, or simply enjoy the company of friends and family, our Clubhouse provides a warm and welcoming atmosphere that makes every gathering memorable. Experience the joy of leisure and the delight of shared moments at the centerpiece of your resort stay.

Kids Play Area

…Where Fun and Adventure Await Little Explorers…

Our vibrant and secure Kids' Play Area is designed to be a haven of fun and imagination, where children can embark on countless adventures and explore their creativity. Spanning a spacious and well-equipped environment, the play area features a variety of activities that cater to different age groups and interests. From brightly colored slides and climbing structures to interactive games and soft play zones, there is something to captivate every child's attention and keep them entertained for hours. Our friendly and attentive staff are always on hand to ensure safety and provide guidance, allowing parents to relax and enjoy their own leisure time, knowing that their little ones are in a safe and stimulating environment.

Beyond just fun and games, our Kids' Play Area is a place where friendships are forged, and new skills are discovered. With dedicated sections for arts and crafts.

Pet-Friendly

…Where Pets Play, Stay, and Feel Loved…

Our pet-friendly accommodations ensure that your pets can relax and enjoy the beautiful surroundings just as much as you do. With ample outdoor spaces perfect for long walks and playful exploration, your pets will have plenty of opportunities to stretch their legs and enjoy the fresh air. We have designated pet-friendly areas where they can safely roam and play, and you’ll find scenic trails and open spaces ideal for shared adventures. Whether you’re exploring the resort’s lush grounds or simply unwinding in your room, your pets are welcome to be part of your memorable getaway.

Fitness Centre

…Stay Fit in Paradise…

The Fitness Centre at the resort is well equipped with Treadmills, Bicycles, Weight Stations, Free Weights, Elliptical Cross Trainers, Exercise balls and mats. These equipment can energize and refresh you throughout the stay at Wayanad Silverwoods.

The Fitness Centre in the resort is large and spacious with several machines on offer. There are many yoga, Pilates and other classes to choose from...

Silverwoods is the top-rated Resorts with Fitness Centre in Wayanad and a scrupulously clean and extremely comfortable gym for fast, convenient, and affordable workout. The Fitness Centre is open everyday, and is a great place to come and relax after a long day exploring the area.

The Fitness Centre is well-equipped with a variety of cardio machines, free weights, and body weight equipment.

Coffee Shop

…Serenity at Lakeside Brews…

Step into a haven of aroma where the rich scent of freshly brewed coffee mingles with the tantalizing aroma of baked pastries. Whether you're starting your day with a steaming cup of artisanal coffee or indulging in a mid-afternoon pick-me-up, our expert baristas are dedicated to crafting the perfect brew to suit your taste buds.Savor every sip in the warm ambiance of our coffee shop, where plush seating invites you to linger over your favorite beverage. With a selection of premium blends sourced from around the world, our coffee shop is the ideal spot to unwind and indulge in a moment of pure relaxation during your stay at our resort.

Bird Watching

…Nature's Winged Treasures…

Embark on a captivating journey into the realm of avian wonders at our resort's dedicated bird-watching sanctuary. our carefully curated habitat provides the perfect vantage point to observe a diverse array of bird species in their natural habitat. Whether you're an avid birder or a casual observer, our expert guides are on hand to lead you through lush forests and serene lakeshores, offering fascinating insights into the behaviors and habitats of the local bird population. As the sun rises over the majestic mountains and glistens on the calm surface of the nearby lakes, immerse yourself in the symphony of bird calls echoing through the air.

Transporation

…Safe & Secure Transportation Services…

At our resort, we take the hassle out of parking with our exceptional valet service. From the moment you arrive, our courteous and professional staff will greet you and ensure your vehicle is safely parked and ready whenever you need it. In addition to valet parking, our resort offers a comprehensive transportation service to make your travels seamless and stress-free. Our fleet of comfortable and well-maintained vehicles is at your disposal, providing convenient transfers to and from nearby attractions and local points of interest. Whether you're planning a scenic drive through the mountains or a relaxing day at the lake, our transportation service ensures that you reach your destination with ease and comfort, enhancing your overall resort experience.
